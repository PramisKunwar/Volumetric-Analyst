Equivalent Weight

E = Atomic mass / Valency (for elements)

E = Molar mass / n-factor (for compounds)

Typen-factor Rule
AcidBasicity (no. of replaceable H⁺)
BaseAcidity (no. of OH⁻ ions)
SaltTotal +ve charge on cation
OxidisingTotal electrons gained
ReducingTotal electrons lost
RadicalTotal charge on radical

Concentration Units

M = moles of solute / L of solution

N = gram equivalents / L of solution

m = moles of solute / kg of solvent

g/L = mass(g) / volume(L)

%w/v = mass(g) / 100 mL solution

%w/w = mass(g) / 100 g solution

ppm = parts per million (mg/L)

ppb = parts per billion (µg/L)

Key Relations

N = M × n-factor

g/L = %w/v × 10

M = g/L ÷ Molar mass

ppb = ppm × 1000

Normality Equation

N₁V₁ = N₂V₂

M₁n₁V₁ = M₂n₂V₂

Dilution

M₁V₁ = M₂V₂ (moles remain constant)
